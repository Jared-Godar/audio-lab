# Membership Has Requirements: Adult ADHD, Told Straight

You got the adult ADHD diagnosis - here's your orientation, told straight. A host and an expert who has it too walk through what the peer-reviewed evidence actually says: it's ~74% heritable, older than the United States, genuinely dangerous untreated, and one of the most treatable things in psychiatry. Dark humour, real citations, no woo.
